BMUCO

BMUCO is a nonprofit theoretical science + AI lab and global science organisation that I co-founded in 2019 with a group of like-minded university students. We build programs across research mentorship, winter schools, and public talks — and have established partnerships with leading institutions across Europe.

Our model is to bring the most talented students into direct contact with frontier research and researchers, early.

Current Flagship Initiative

Lean 4 · Active

Graduate Proof Corpus for Lean 4

BMUCO is building the first structured student pipeline for expert-verified, graduate-level formal mathematics — a proof corpus in Lean 4 designed to train the next generation of AI reasoning models.

We run a Directed Reading Programme (DRP) followed by intensive workshops at Bonn, producing human-verified proofs at graduate level. This dataset addresses a critical gap: current AI training data for mathematics barely reaches undergraduate level.
